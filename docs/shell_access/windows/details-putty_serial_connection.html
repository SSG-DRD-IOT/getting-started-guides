Establish a serial connection

Use PuTTY to establish a serial connection to the IoT board using the COM # you noted in the previous Identify the serial COM port section and the baud rate 115200.

  1. In PuTTY, double-check that you are in the “Session” screen.

  1. Select the “Serial” radio button under “Connection type”.

  1. Specify the destination you want to connect to:
  • Serial Line: use the COM # (e.g. “COM3”) noted in Identify the serial COM port
  • Speed: use “115200” for the baud rate

  1. Click “Open” to connect to the board.

  1. When you see a blank screen, press the Enter key.

For Intel® Edison boards running older firmware: You may need to press the Enter key twice.

  1. Once connected you will see a login prompt.

Type in “root” for the username and press Enter.
